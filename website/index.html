HEALTH ANALYZER

Know the Future and Monitor the Present

The Internet of Things (IoT) has the potential of changing the healthcare landscape. When diagnosis can be achieved early and accurately, lives can saved. Through the use of smart phones and small sensor-based wearable technology, user symptoms can be monitored, analyzed and diagnosed easily and fast. In this project, we propose to collect sensor data (through simulation) and send updates to users regarding their health status. We plan to collect the data for the following health parameters pulse rate, blood pressure and body temperature. Any fluctuation in the data coming in can be reported to the user via SMS. Further, we would be predicting the chance of heart attack leveraging machine learning algorithms.
